Broth rice and chicken

This recipe is an easy and tasty way of making chicken and rice

These are the things you will need:

  • Chicken breast (as much as you like)
  • A Stock cube
  • Water
  • Rice

Cooking it:

Depending on what your stock cube manufacturer says, pour as much water in a pan to desolve the cube.

Pour the rice in the pan with the water so you get a 1:2 rice to water ratio (you can of cause clean the rice before cooking).

When the water has reached a boiling temperature, stir it around to distribute the stock.

While you cook the rice, you can fry the chicken in a separate pan.

Depending on the rice and chicken, both should be done cooking at the same time.
